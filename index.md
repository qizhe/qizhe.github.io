      <p>
        I am an Assistant Professor in the
        <a href="https://engineering.virginia.edu/departments/computer-science">Department of Computer Science</a>
        at the University of Virginia.
      </p>

      <p>
        I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from
        <a href="https://www.cs.cornell.edu/">Cornell University</a>,
        where I was advised by
        <a href="https://www.cs.cornell.edu/~ragarwal/">Rachit Agarwal</a>.
        Before that, I earned my Master’s degree from
        <a href="https://www.cs.princeton.edu/">Princeton University</a>,
        where I was advised by
        <a href="https://www.cs.princeton.edu/~jrex/">Jennifer Rexford</a>.
        I completed my undergraduate studies in computer science at the
        <a href="https://cse.engin.umich.edu/">University of Michigan</a>.
      </p>

      <p>
        My research broadly spans computer networks, operating systems, and computer hardware.
      </p>

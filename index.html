About

I am a final year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Cornell University. I am advised by Rachit Agarwal. Before joing Cornell, I got my Master of Science degree in the Department of Computer Science at Princeton University,
advised by Jennifer Rexford. I got my Bachelor degree in Computer Science from department of EECS at University of Michigan.

My current research interest is designing and building network stacks/protocols/hardwares for Terabit Ethernet.

I will join the University of Virginia as an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science in Fall 2025!

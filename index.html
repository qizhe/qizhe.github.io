About

I am a second year master student in the Department of Computer Science at Princeton University. I am very fortunate to be advised by Jennifer Rexford. Previously, I got my Bachelor degree in Computer Science from department of EECS at University of Michigan.

My current research interests are SDN, NFV(and Middleboxes), Mobile Computing, Computer Security, IoT and Distributed System. For SDN, I am particularly interested in utilizing programmable switches to do network monitoring and design new routing algorithms or traffic engineering algorithm.
